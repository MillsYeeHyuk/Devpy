<p>In <a href="/wiki/Machine_learning" title="Machine learning">machine learning</a>, a <b>convolutional neural network</b> (<b>CNN</b>, or <b>ConvNet</b>) is a class of deep, <a href="/wiki/Feedforward_neural_network" title="Feedforward neural network">feed-forward</a> <a href="/wiki/Artificial_neural_network" title="Artificial neural network">artificial neural network</a> that have successfully been applied to analyzing visual imagery.</p>
